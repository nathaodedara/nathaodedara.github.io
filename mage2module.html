Adobe Official Marketplace Modules

Telephone Validation

The Telephone Validation extension adds international telephone validation for all countries and also adds a country flag to the telephone field. It also detects and sets the user's countries automatically based on their GEO IP location.

The Magento 2 custom extension adds intuitive functionality to the telephone field for international validation based on the selected country. With this extension, customers can easily input their phone numbers in the correct format based on their location, thereby reducing errors and ensuring accurate communication between merchants and customers. Additionally, the extension also adds country flags to the telephone field, making it easier for customers to identify the correct country code for their location. This feature enhances the user experience and improves the overall aesthetic appeal of the checkout process. By incorporating these features, the Magento 2 custom extension streamlines the checkout process and helps merchants provide a more user-friendly and efficient shopping experience to their customers.

Improved Checkout

The Improved Checkout extension helps merchants to customize their checkout addresses and gives them the functionality to move their addresses to the shipping step below the shipping address.

Having a neat and clean checkout process is a very important portion of any merchant site and making it easier and faster for customers at the end helps merchants in better sales and revenue. As it gives functionality to move the billing address below the shipping address, it overall makes it easy for the end customers as they can decide and input accordingly for both addresses at one place and in the next step, all they have to worry about is only the payment portion.

The extension will show the billing address option right after the shipping address. In other words, it will be easy for end customers to opt for their shipping and billing addresses altogether in one place. This functionality has a great impact on the entire checkout page usability. By creating positive user interface merchants will get a higher conversion rate on the checkout page.
