Magento 2 Modules

Pop Up Notice For Magento 2

The module Popupnotice, help users in showing any type of announcement or notice to the users in frontend. Users can opt to dismiss the notification bar from the screen and it will not be displayed again until there is a new notice. Thus, also making a convenient experience for both frontend users and sellers.

Questions as stored seperately for each products.
